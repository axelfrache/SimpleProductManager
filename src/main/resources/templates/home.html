Welcome to Stock Pilot

This tool is designed to simplify the management of your products, orders, clients, and categories.

About Product Manager

Product Manager allows you to:

  • Manage your product inventory.
  • Track and manage your clients' orders.
  • Organize your products into categories.
  • Keep your clients' information up to date.

Getting Started

To start using Product Manager:

  1. Go to the products section to add or edit products.
  2. Use the orders section to view recent orders or add new ones.
  3. Manage your clients in the dedicated section.
  4. Organize your products more efficiently by assigning them to categories.
